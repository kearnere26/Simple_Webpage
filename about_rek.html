About Us

History:

Final Fantasy BobaTea started three years ago at the height of popularity for Japanese style games, art, and animation. We serve all kinds of customers whether interested in Japanese culture or not. We have always prided ourselves in the level of customer service that we provide each an every customer. There are currently four other shops located in San Francisco, Austin, Houston, and New York city.

Mission Statement:

Hello customers welcome to Final Fantasy BobaTea, we serve everyone in the community. Our goal is to provide a friendly and inclusive environment for not only fans of Final Fantasy but to also those who may not be fans. We encourage everyone to learn the lore of Final Fantasy a nd why it has impacted so many. Stop by and enjoy a delicious treat!

Important People of Final Fantasy BobaTea:

Name: Naoki Yoshida

Title: Director

Naoki Yoshida, also known by the nickname Yoshi-P, is a Japanese video game producer, director and designer of Final Fantasy BobaTea.

Name: Masayoshi Soken

Title: President

Masayoshi Soken is a Japanese video game composer and sound editor. Soken is best known for being the lead composer and sound director of Final Fantasy XIV and its expansions. Now the president of our franchise.

Name: Hironobu Sakaguchi

Title: Creator

Hironobu Sakaguchi is a Japanese game designer, director, producer and writer. He is known as the creator of the Final Fantasy franchise which lead to Final Fantasy BobaTea.
